Chicken Bucatini with Creamy Garlic, Sun-Dried Tomato and Spinach Sauce

Description

A favorite in our household, this recipe is a savory symphony of tender chicken, al dente pasta, and rich, velvety sauce bursting with the flavors of garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, and spinach. It is a culinary delight that satisfies both the palate and the soul. Uses multiple DeLallo ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. package of DeLallo Bucatini
  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (6.7 oz.) jar of DeLallo Sun-Dried Tomatoes, drained and roughly chopped
  • 2 cups chopped spinach
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cups grated DeLallo Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Season both sides of chicken breasts with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken breasts 5 minutes per side or until cooked through. Remove from heat and set aside. Slice or cube once cool.
  4. In the same pan, add the butter and minced garlic. Once fragrant, add the sun-dried tomatoes and spinach. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat and add in the heavy cream and Parmigiano. Whisk until fully incorporated and creamy.
  6. Add the chicken back to the pan, then stir in pasta. Add some reserved pasta water a little at a time to achieve desired consistency.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
